Wakaflocka Flame Wants Your help!

Donate Now

Help kids achieve their dreams

Wakaflocka Flame here. Rah Rah’s Tech Truck connects technology and kids (in underprivileged hoods) to help kids achieve their dreams.

In honor of my brother, we are visiting cities, bringing technology to kids to help them follow their passions in music, coding, film and more and learn other life skills (like how to setup a bank account or email address) to help break the chain of underachievement. Donate today to help kids achieve their dreams. .
